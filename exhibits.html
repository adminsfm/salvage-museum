Bessie's Sewing Closet

The under-stair closet remembers Bessie Heffern's sewing nook through her original sewing machine alongside textiles. It also features poetry written by the women of Salvage.

From the Cradle to the Grave

Upstairs, a replica of a bedroom featuring a wooden cradle, clothing, hats, jewellery, a feather bed, and the Shroud of Salvage speak to family life and death customs.

Community Institutions

The Bishop's Harbour room and hallway include artifacts and archives on schools, church organizations, commercial activity, hunting and fishing, leisure, the FPU, and the Loyal Orange Lodge.

Self-Guided Tour

A self-guided tour is available at the museum. Staff and volunteers are happy to answer questions, help with church records or archival resources, and point visitors toward exhibits that match their interests.
